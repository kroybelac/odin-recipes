Cream Cheese Pasta

Description

This recipe is quick with only a few ingredients, perfect for a side to any many course.

Ingredients

  • 8oz spaghetti
  • 4oz cream cheese, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup reserved pasta water
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. In a large pot, cook pasta. Save 1/2 of water. Drain pasta.
  2. Retun pot to heat over medium low and add reserved water and cream cheese.
  3. Whisk until smooth. Add garlic power. Add salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add pasta back to pot and toss to coat. Serve.
