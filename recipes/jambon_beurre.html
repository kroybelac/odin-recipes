Jambon-beurre

Description

The Jambon-beurre is a simple, yet delicious sandwich made with ham and butter, a favorite of the French where they are said to eat over some 3 million sandwiches a day.

Ingredients

  • 2 french baguettes
  • european butter
  • parisian ham or any other high-quality ham
  • cruyere
  • cornichons

Directions

  1. Slice baguette in half and cut not quite through horizontally
  2. Slather a ridiculous amount of butter on the baguette.
  3. Lay slices of Gruyere on bottom of bagguette
  4. Fold ham over itself, covering the sandwich
  5. Drop a few cornichons on top of ham.
