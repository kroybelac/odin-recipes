Chicago-Style Hot Dog

Description

The Chicago-Style Hot dog is an iconic piece of Chicago's street food. "Dragged through the garden" as some would say, this dog has no shortage of toppings, making it a beautiful, delicious mess.

Ingredients

  • 1 poppy seed bun
  • 1 all-beef beef hot dog
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 1 tbsp "neon" green relish
  • 2 tomato wedges
  • 1 pickle spear
  • 2 sport peppers
  • 1 tbsp white onion, chopped
  • 1 dash celery salt

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil. Turn heat to low and drop in hot dog. Cook for five minutes or until hot.
  2. Place steamer on top of pot and place in your poppy seed bun. Steam two minutes or until warm
  3. Pile on ingredients in this order: yellow mustard, sweet green pickle relish, onion, tomato wedges, pickle spear, sport peppers, and celery salt.
  4. The tomatoes should be nestled between the hot dog and the top of the bun.
  5. Place the pickle between the hot dog and the bottom of the bun.
